BOOKS

May, 21, 2022

गवर्नेंस का राइट ऐंगल

Released by Delhi CM Shri Arvind Kejriwal along with Delhi Assembly speaker Shri Ram Niwas ji Goel on 21 May 2022 at CM house in Delhi in presence of others.

Written on the occasion of 75 years of Indian freedom, the book analyzes the Governance rendered by different political parties in power from the point of view of a common man and try to find answers likes of What an Indian Common man have got it? Was today India, what our freedom fighter had dreamed of India?

January, 24, 2021

मधुसुदन दयाल संपादक

Released by Delhi Dy CM Shri Manish Sishodiya on 24th January 2021 in a function at SSV (PG) College, Hapur (UP)

This book is based on the biography of Late Madhusudan Dayal editor, his thoughts influenced by Mahatma Gandhi, Subhash Chandra Bose, Ram Manohar Lohia and Journalist Ganesh Shankar Vidyarti, attempt to cover the background of Hapur region of Western Uttar Pradesh and its historical importance In which India's freedom struggle, politics of independent India, changing journalism and education, as well as the history of migration from small towns and cities to metros is also recorded.
